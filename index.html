Shubh Villa 'Festival & Celebrations'.

Following festival were identified for celebration in society

1. Paush Bada (Any date between 1st of JAN to 14th JAN) [Menu Halwa+Bada+Puri+Sabji for colony members and dona Prasadi for others]

2. Republic day: Flag hoisting and laddu distribution

3. Mahashivaratri: Mandir decoration and Pooja

4. Holi Milan: Combined Holi celebration (Color and Thandai to be arranged by cultural committee)+Dinner+ DJ

5. Ramnavmi Chetra: decoration and panchamrat and dona prasadi

6. Van mahotsav : Tree plantation by kids in colony aur near by area in first week of July

7. Janmashtami : Mandir decoration +Panchaarat and dona prasadi

8. Independence day: Flag hoisting and laddu distribution

9. Ganesh Chaturthi : Ganesh Sthapna for 1 day and visarjan with DJ

10. Vijayadashmi/Dussehara : Rawan Dahan

11. Dipawali Milan : Annakut mhotsav within 15 days from diwali
